About Us

JB Sushi Bar

JB Sushi Bar, nestled in the heart of London, is a culinary gem that combines the artistry of Japanese cuisine with a contemporary flair. With a menu brimming with a delectable array of sushi, sashimi, and other Japanese delights, it's a haven for both sushi enthusiasts and newcomers to this gastronomic world. Their sushi chefs craft each roll with precision and creativity, using the freshest ingredients available. The restaurant's serene ambiance provides the perfect backdrop for a memorable dining experience, whether you're enjoying a romantic evening out or gathering with friends. JB Sushi Bar offers a taste of Japan in the vibrant setting of London, making it a must-visit destination for anyone seeking an authentic and delightful sushi experience.

Perfect Ingredients

Our commitment to excellence is most evident in the delicacies we present on your plate. Every ingredient, meticulously sourced from trusted suppliers, is a testament to our dedication to freshness and quality. We believe that exceptional sushi starts with the finest components, and we spare no effort in ensuring that the flavors we serve are unparalleled. From hand-selected, high-quality fish to thoughtfully chosen accompaniments, each roll and piece of nigiri embodies our devotion to the art of sushi-making. In addition to our culinary offerings, we've curated a selection of beverages that complement our dishes flawlessly. With a range of choices that includes a variety of sake, as well as an assortment of wines, gins, and other spirits with a Japanese connection, your dining experience is elevated to a symphony of flavors.

Meet the Master Chef

Behind every exceptional meal is an extraordinary chef, and ours brings over two decades of sushi expertise to your table. Having honed his craft across Europe, our master chef infuses his creations with a touch of traditional Japanese techniques along with innovative twists that appeal to the modern palate. His most recent stop was in the bustling culinary scene of London, where he perfected his skills and refined his art. At our sushi shop, his expertise takes center stage, offering you an unforgettable culinary experience that's steeped in tradition yet guided by a vision for the future. Visit us to embark on a sushi adventure that transcends the ordinary, where each moment is a harmonious blend of taste, ambiance, and mastery.
